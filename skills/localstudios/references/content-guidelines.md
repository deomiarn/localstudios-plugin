# Content Writing Guidelines

## Per-Page SEO Requirements

### H1 Rules
- Exactly ONE H1 per page
- H1 contains the primary keyword of that page
- H1 contains the location/city name
- H1 is the first heading on the page
- H1 is descriptive, not just the keyword (good: "Expert Dental Care in Zurich", bad: "Dentist Zurich")

### H2 Rules
- H2s contain secondary keywords and geo-terms
- H2s are naturally phrased, not keyword-stuffed
- At least one H2 includes a geo-term
- Use 3-6 H2s per page depending on content length

### Meta Tags (every page)
- **Meta Title**: Primary KW — Company — City (max 60 characters)
- **Meta Description**: Concrete benefit + call to action + location (max 155 characters)
- **OG Title**: Same as meta title or slightly more engaging variant
- **OG Description**: Same as meta description or social-friendly variant
- Both OG tags are critical for WhatsApp/LinkedIn/social sharing previews

### First Paragraph Rule
- First paragraph must contain:
  - The primary keyword
  - The city/location name
  - A clear statement of what the business does
- Keep it concise (2-3 sentences)
- This paragraph often becomes the Google snippet

## E-E-A-T Content Quality

### Experience
- Write from first-person perspective where appropriate ("We have been serving...")
- Include specific details only someone with experience would know
- Reference real processes, tools, or methods used

### Expertise
- Mention qualifications, certifications, training
- Use correct industry terminology (but explain it for readers)
- Reference standards or best practices in the field

### Authoritativeness
- Include years of experience, number of clients served
- Reference awards, memberships, partnerships
- Link to authoritative external sources where relevant

### Trust
- Display full NAP consistently across all pages
- Mention Google reviews or ratings
- Include real team member names where possible
- Reference local community involvement

## FAQ Section Rules (Service Pages)

- 5-7 questions per service page
- Phrased as people actually speak/search (voice-search ready):
  - Good: "How much does teeth whitening cost in Zurich?"
  - Bad: "Teeth whitening pricing"
- Each answer: 2-4 sentences, directly answering the question
- Include the service name and location in at least 2 answers
- FAQ feeds directly into FAQPage schema markup

## CTA Placement Rules

1. **Hero section** (above the fold): Primary CTA button
   - Action-oriented text: "Book Appointment", "Get Free Quote", "Call Now"
   - Phone number visible (clickable tel: link)
2. **After social proof**: Secondary CTA
   - Reinforces the action after trust has been built
3. **End of page** (before footer): Final CTA
   - Last chance to convert before they leave

## Content Length Guidelines
- Home: 600-1000 words
- About: 400-700 words
- Service pages: 800-1200 words (including FAQ)
- Contact: 200-400 words (focused on NAP and form)

## Writing Tone
- Match the tone discovered in scraping, or default to:
  - Professional but approachable
  - Clear, not jargon-heavy
  - Confident but not aggressive
  - Local and personal, not corporate
- Adapt to the industry (medical = more formal, creative = more casual)

## Things to AVOID
- Generic phrases: "Welcome to our website", "We are a leading..."
- Keyword stuffing or unnatural keyword placement
- Duplicate content across pages
- Walls of text without headings
- Missing calls to action
- "Click here" as anchor text
- Placeholder text left in final content
